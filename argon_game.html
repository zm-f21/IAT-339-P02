"Argon Chalice: Spirits of the Forest" is an adventure platformer centered around a wizard who must save an enchanted forest ruled by the spirits of the forest. When the spirits escape the chalice, chaos in ensued in the forest. The wizard is called upon by the keeper of the forest to restore order and return the spirits back to the chalice.
