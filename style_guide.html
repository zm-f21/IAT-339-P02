    Basic Branding Ideas


    What does the company do, or offer?

    The company is a game development company, creating games and putting them out on platforms. They also offer merchandise related to the games they have.

    Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

    The potential customers are gamers from different audiences based on the game they are interested in. Some games are made for younger audiences but can also be enjoyed by any age.

    Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

    If someone is a fan of a game, they might want to support the devlopment of more games from the company with a purchase of the company's merchandise.

    Potential Imagery




    Since the company is trying to advertise the games they made, instead of using icons or external imagery, illustrations from each respective game will be used instead.

    Characters from the games will be sprinkled around to accessorize a page, accompanied with text (doesn't have to be related).
